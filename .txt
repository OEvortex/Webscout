## 🎯 KEY INFO

**The single best "everything" backend**: https://pollinations.ai — powers ~500 community apps, free image/video/audio/text, no signup needed for basic use. Image models: flux, gptimage, kontext, seedream, nanobanana, zimage, wan-image, qwen-image, grok-imagine. Text models include openai, claude-sonnet-4.5, claude-opus-4.5, gemini-3-flash, deepseek-v3, kimi-k2.

**Most reliable "no-login" LLM**: Duck.ai — supports GPT-4o mini, Claude 3 Haiku, Llama 3.3 70B, Mistral Small 3. Chats not stored, not used for training.

**GitHub index sources** (for ongoing updates):
- https://github.com/zebbern/no-cost-ai (80+ services, no signup)
- https://github.com/rocnubie/Awesome-AI-Websites-2026 (curated 2026 list)
- https://github.com/T-R-I-T-E-J/awesome-free-ai-resources (290+ tools)
- https://github.com/aviaryan/awesome-no-login-web-apps (general no-login)
- https://github.com/OuterSpacee/free-ai-apis (100+ APIs, 15 cats)

**Privacy gold**: OptiPix.art, Wordcast.app, EarScribe.app, Quick-TTS.com — run 100% in browser, your data never leaves.

**Watch out**: most "free AI image" sites are wrappers around Pollinations or Stable Horde — quality varies, daily caps exist, and "unlimited" usually means "rate-limited per IP". Treat prompts as public.
